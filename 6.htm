Share access

On Aternos you can share your server with your friends without giving them direct access to your account. This feature is called "Access", you can find it in the panel in the server navigation: aternos.org/access/

Grant a friend access to your server

Enter your friend's Aternos username into the text box on the access settings page and click the plus sign.

If your friend does not have an Aternos account yet they need to create one here: aternos.org/signup/ 

You'll see a list of permissions which you can grant individually to each friend.

Linking an email address to your account

You can optionally add an email address to your account to be able to reset your password. The same email address can be used for multiple Aternos accounts.
Accounts created using a Google account cannot be linked to an additional email address.

Important:
If you choose not to add an email address, it is not possible to reset your account's password. You either need to remember the correct password for your account, or your account can no longer be accessed.

We will only use your email address for this one purpose and never send you newsletters or anything like that. If you want to learn more about the handling of email addresses and other personal data on Aternos, you should read our privacy policy.

If you just want to start with your server from scratch you don't need to close your account. Just reinstall your server by clicking the reinstall button on the options page.

If you really want us to delete all your data, you can close your Aternos account here. After confirming account deletion your account will be disabled for seven days. Within this time, you can still log in to your account in order to reactivate it.

After seven days your account will be permanently deleted. Please be aware that this will delete your entire account and all server files forever.
